About Me

I am a trained Architectural Technologist with practical experience in architectural design, construction coordination, and site supervision. My background includes working on residential buildings and infrastructure-related projects, where I have supported project execution through accurate technical drawings, material coordination, and compliance with health and safety standards.

I am skilled in preparing and interpreting 2D and 3D drawings using industry-standard tools such as AutoCAD, Revit, and SketchUp, ensuring design intent is clearly communicated from planning to construction stages. My work focuses on accuracy, functionality, and adherence to approved specifications.

In addition to physical construction support, I also provide digital and remote services including technical documentation, online project assistance, and ethical promotion of third-party digital products and services. This blended approach allows me to collaborate with clients and partners both locally and remotely.

I value professionalism, transparency, and continuous learning, and I aim to deliver dependable services that meet project requirements while maintaining industry best practices. This website serves as a professional profile and verification platform for partnerships, service inquiries, and online collaborations.
